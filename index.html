About the App

Fun Pal is your ultimate companion for parties, family nights, and friendly gatherings! With two exciting modes — Party Mode and Family Mode — Fun Pal brings laughter, energy, and connection to any event. Enjoy a wide range of fun, family-friendly games, including:

  • Truth or Dare – Break the ice with lighthearted truths and dares suitable for all ages.
  • Never Have I Ever – Discover fun facts about your friends and family without any adult content.
  • Card Games – Classic and original card-based mini-games made easy for everyone to enjoy.
  • Dice Games – Roll the dice and complete playful challenges with a twist.

Fun Pal also features a point system to add a friendly competitive edge — earn points for completing challenges and answering questions to see who comes out on top!

Whether you're hosting a celebration, a family night, or a casual hangout, Fun Pal makes sure everyone has a great time — with no alcohol involved.

Frequently Asked Questions

1. Is Fun Pal free to use?

No, Fun Pal requires a subscription to access its content. We offer three subscription options: weekly, monthly, and annual plans. Our monthly and annual subscriptions come with a 3-day free trial so you can explore the app before committing.

2. Can I play Fun Pal offline?

Yes! Once downloaded and subscribed, all Fun Pal games and modes are fully available offline. No internet connection is required to play after installation and activation.

3. I'm experiencing a bug. What should I do?

If you encounter any issues, please email us with a description of the problem, your device type, and app version. We'll work to fix it as quickly as possible.

4. How does the point system work?

In Fun Pal, players can earn points by completing challenges, answering questions, and participating in games. The player with the most points at the end wins bragging rights! It's a fun way to add a little competition to your gathering.

5. How do I manage or cancel my subscription?

All subscriptions are managed through Apple. You can cancel or manage your subscription anytime by going to your iOS device's Settings > Apple ID > Subscriptions.
