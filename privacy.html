Privacy Policy

Fun Pal (“we”, “our”, or “us”) is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information when you use our mobile application (the “App”).

By using the App, you acknowledge that you have read and understood this Privacy Policy.

1. Information We Collect

Fun Pal does not collect or store personal information. Any player names, game settings, or scores you enter are saved locally on your device and are never transmitted to us or any external server. Subscription management is handled securely by our partner RevenueCat. RevenueCat may collect anonymous purchase information (such as subscription status) to enable access to Fun Pal's content. We do not access or store this information ourselves.

2. No Analytics or Tracking

We do not use cookies, analytics tools, advertising networks, or any form of cross-app or third-party tracking. All usage happens locally on your device.

3. Data Storage

All game data and player information is stored locally on your device using secure storage methods. We do not maintain any external databases of user information.

4. Data Security

Fun Pal keeps your gameplay data private. Your player names and scores stay on your device. Subscription access is handled securely through RevenueCat.

5. Third-Party Services

Fun Pal uses a trusted third-party service, RevenueCat, to manage subscriptions and access to premium content. RevenueCat may process anonymous purchase information (such as receipts and subscription status) to enable your access to Fun Pal features. We do not directly collect or store personal information through Fun Pal, and we do not share your personal information with any other external parties.

6. Children’s Privacy

Fun Pal offers a Family Mode designed for users of all ages, including children. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13.

7. Changes to Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy to reflect changes to our practices or for legal or regulatory reasons. Any updates will be posted within the App or on the website.
